How It Works

Three steps to better chess

No account needed. Your data stays on your machine.

1

Import your games

Connect your Lichess or Chess.com username. Blunder Tutor fetches all your games automatically.

2

Find your blunders

Stockfish analyzes every move and identifies the positions where you went wrong — blunders, mistakes, inaccuracies.

3

Practice as puzzles

Each blunder becomes a puzzle. Drill the exact positions where you made mistakes until you stop repeating them.

Features

Everything you need to improve

Multi-Platform Import

Pull games from Lichess and Chess.com into one place. All time controls, all game types.

Puzzle Trainer

Your blunders become interactive puzzles with hints, best-move arrows, and move validation on a real board.

Analytics Dashboard

Accuracy trends, blunder heatmaps, opening breakdown, game-phase distribution. See exactly where you're weakest.

Auto-Sync

Scheduled background fetching and analysis. New games are imported and analyzed automatically.

Stockfish 18 Analysis

Configurable analysis depth with the strongest open-source chess engine. Runs entirely on your hardware.

Self-Hosted & Private

Your data never leaves your machine. No accounts, no tracking, no subscriptions. Just Docker and go.

Quick Start

Up and running in 30 seconds

# Run with Docker — that's all you need
$ docker run -p 8000:8000 \
    -v $(pwd)/data:/app/data \
    ghcr.io/mrlokans/blunder-tutor:latest

Then open http://localhost:8000 and enter your chess username.
For Docker Compose and more options, see the deployment guide.
